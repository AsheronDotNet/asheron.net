Option 1: Use your current acclient.exe file

Make a shortcut of your current acclient.exe file > Right click shortcut > Click 'Properties'. Under 'Shortcut' tab add -h [Address]:[Port] -a username:password outside of the quotes of your acclient.exe location. Change [Address] to one of the following servers and [Port] to its corresponding port. (Example: "C:\Games\Asheron's Call\acclient.exe" -h 75.63.120.231:9050 -a JohnDoe:pass1) View all servers
